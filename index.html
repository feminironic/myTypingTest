About Meichn Typing Test

How It Works

Test your typing speed with space-themed passages. Choose your difficulty level and test duration, then type the displayed text as accurately and quickly as possible.

Difficulty Levels

  • Novice: Simple space facts with basic vocabulary. Perfect for beginners or casual typists.
  • Pilot: Moderate complexity with longer sentences about space exploration. Good for intermediate typists.
  • Commander: Advanced vocabulary and complex sentences about astrophysics concepts. Challenging for most typists.
  • Cosmic Master: Expert level with scientific terminology and intricate concepts about cosmology. Only for the most skilled typists.

Test Durations

Choose from 15 seconds (quick test), 30 seconds (brief test), 1 minute (standard test), 2 minutes (extended test), or 5 minutes (endurance test).

Scoring

  • WPM (Words Per Minute): Measures your typing speed.
  • Accuracy: The percentage of characters typed correctly.
